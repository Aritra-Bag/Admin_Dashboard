Administrative Expenses Analysis

June - August 2025

Key Insights

  • Overall Trend: Total expenses dipped from ₹2.31Cr in June to ₹2.16Cr in July, before rising to nearly ₹2.3Cr in August.
  • Top Cost Drivers: 'REGO ETS Services' and 'Canteen' are consistently the highest expenses, together accounting for over ₹1.03Cr in August alone.
  • Significant Increase: 'Hotel and Restaurant Charges' saw a major increase of over 41% between June and August.
  • Volatile Spending: 'Courier Services' costs fluctuated significantly, increasing by 40% in July before dropping back down in August.

Discussion Point

With 'REGO ETS' and 'Canteen' services consistently making up a large portion of admin expenses, we should prioritize a review of these high-value contracts to identify potential cost-saving measures.
