Monthly Expense Overview & Control

February - August 2025

Total Monthly Admin Expense

Expense Control: Target vs. Achieve

Summary & Discussion

Key Observations

  • A significant spike in expenses occurred in June, rising from ₹1.80 Cr in May to ₹2.30 Cr.
  • Since the June peak, there has been a consistent, slight downward trend in monthly expenses.
  • Positive performance against targets, with actual expenses coming in under budget for both July and August.

Discussion Points

  • The spike in June was concentrated in a few large line items: REGO ETS Services, Security Services and Facility Management, which together formed nearly half of the monthly spend.
  • Cost-control measures implemented between June and July succeeded in pulling down total spend by ~6.3% in July.
