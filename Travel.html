Travel Expenses Analysis

February - July 2025

Key Insights

  • April Expense Spike: Total travel expenses peaked sharply in April at ₹9.34M, primarily driven by a surge in flight costs to over ₹7M.
  • Flight Dominance: Air travel consistently represents the largest portion of travel expenses, accounting for over 58% of the total spend across the six months.
  • Lowest Spend in March: March saw the lowest travel expenditure at ₹4.99M, a significant dip before the April peak.
  • Hotel Cost Variability: Hotel expenses show high variability, ranging from ₹649K in May to over ₹1.75M in April.

Discussion Point

The significant expenditure spike in April, particularly in flights and hotels, suggests a major event or project. We forecast similar peaks in the future, understanding the business drivers for this increased travel, to better manage the travel budget.
