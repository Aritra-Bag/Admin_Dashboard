Cost Control & Optimization Initiatives

Actions Taken to Enhance Efficiency and Reduce Spend

Travel Cost Control

  • Standardized Booking Process: A mandatory travel form has been introduced to streamline and control all travel bookings.
  • Strategic Hotel Onboarding: Partnered with three hotels (two near HO @ ₹2,200/night, one near Airport @ ₹3,000/night) on a BTC basis for cost-effective and convenient stays.
  • Optimized Visa Processing: Migrated to the Atlys portal for visa applications, successfully securing some visas for as low as ₹1 during flash sales.

Transportation Utilization Control

Route-Based Bus Allocation:

Shifted from department-wise to route-wise bus allocation, significantly reducing kilometers traveled and optimizing routes.

Optimized Vehicle Parking:

Relocated bus parking for the Malancha route to RML 7 and Bolero parking to Regent Guest House, minimizing unnecessary travel and improving turnaround times.

Future Plans & Challenges:
  • Negotiations are underway with new vendors for employee transportation.
  • Route-wise deployment will be extended to 24-hour shifts.
  • A 15-day review cycle will be implemented to shuffle buses based on utilization.
  • Challenge: The number of employees in 24-hour shifts currently exceeds available bus capacity.
